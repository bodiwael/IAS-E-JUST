The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) is a key resource for students pursuing careers in electrical, electronics, and industrial engineering.

IAS sister societies focus on specialized technologies that complement IAS’s scope, offering research, networking, and career development resources.

IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES)

The IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) focuses on the generation, transmission, distribution, and storage of electric power. As the world transitions toward renewable energy and smarter grids, PES plays a leading role in developing efficient and sustainable energy solutions.

Learn More

IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES)

Modern industries rely on electronics and automation to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance productivity. The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) is dedicated to advancing industrial automation, robotics, and smart manufacturing technologies.

Learn More

IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS)

Efficient energy conversion is essential for everything from renewable energy systems to electric vehicles. The IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) focuses on the design, control, and application of power electronics to improve energy efficiency and reliability.

Learn More

IEEE Dielectrics and Electrical Insulation Society (DEIS)

Reliable electrical insulation is critical for the safety and performance of electrical systems. The IEEE Dielectrics and Electrical Insulation Society (DEIS) specializes in dielectric materials, insulation technologies, and high-voltage applications.

Learn More

Why Engage with IAS Sister Societies?

  • - Access research, industry insights, and publications.
  • - Attend conferences, workshops, and networking events.
  • - Explore scholarships and mentorship programs.

IEEE and its sister societies provide valuable resources to develop technical skills and build professional networks.
